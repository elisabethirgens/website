Arena

Postedon July 13th, 2015 in Arena by Admin

Arena provides information on securities listed on Oslo Børs, Oslo Axess and N-OTC. There are currently two information products available in Arena: Top Shareholders and Corporate Actions Calendar.

Top Shareholders provides weekly updated lists of the largest shareholders in companies listed on Oslo Børs, Oslo Axess and on the A-list of N-OTC. There are two subscription levels available: TOP20 and TOP30.

Corporate Actions Calendar keeps track of upcoming corporate actions and dividends for shares and primary capital certificates traded on Oslo Børs and shares traded on Oslo Axess.

Arena is developed by Oslo Market Solutions, which is a 100% owned subsidiary by Oslo Børs VPS Holding ASA which develops and operates solutions in security trading. We offer several standard and tailored solutions, Online Equity Trading, Research Publishing and Investor Relations. We are additionally distributing standardized data products based on public information from VPS’ record as well as Oslo Børs.
